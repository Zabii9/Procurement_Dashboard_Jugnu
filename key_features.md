# Key Features — Procurement Intelligence Dashboard

The Procurement Intelligence Dashboard is a high-performance analytics tool designed to optimize inventory levels, manage supplier risk, and streamline working capital. Below is a summary of its core capabilities.

---

### 🚀 1. Strategic Command Center (Overview)
   Real-time KPIs: Instant visibility into Total Purchase Value, Live Inventory Value, and Target Achievement.
   Gap Demand Analytics: Automatically calculates the exact financial value needed to restock the inventory to target levels.
   Out-of-Stock (OOS) Monitoring: Real-time tracking of OOS rates across all segments.

### 🏭 2. Supplier Performance & Risk Matrix
   Risk Evaluation: Scatter plots comparing spend volume against supply reliability (OOS rates).
   Supplier Scorecards: Rank and evaluate manufacturers based on SKU contribution, target compliance, and inventory coverage.
   Concentration Analysis: Visualizes supplier dependency to help diversify procurement and reduce risk.

### 📦 3. Advanced Inventory Health Tracking
   Automated Stock Status: Intelligent categorization of SKUs into Critical, Low, Healthy, and Overstocked based on AWS (Average Weekly Sales).
   Coverage Distribution: Visual analysis of how many weeks of stock are available per SKU.
   AWS vs MSL vs Available: Comparative analysis of current stock against weekly sales trends and minimum stock levels.

### 🎯 4. Precision Target Tracking
   Budget vs Actual: Monitor Month-to-Date (MTD) performance against automated SKU-level targets.
   Compliance Distribution: Breakdowns of SKUs meeting or missing their growth targets.
   Gap Analysis: Identifies specifically which SKUs are underperforming and require sales or procurement intervention.

### 🔮 5. SKU Demand Forecasting (Zero-Sale Intelligence)
   Predictive Replenishment: Identifies demand for core SKUs that have had zero recent sales but are essential for the portfolio.
   Capped Demand Logic: Intelligent ordering logic that prevents over-stocking by capping demand at the target budget.
   Priority Ranking: Automatically flags "P1 Critical" demand for high-contribution SKUs.

### 💼 6. Working Capital & DC Optimization
   Days of Cover (DOC) Planning: Compares current inventory duration against strategic target DOCs.
   Capital Deployment Analysis: Identifies categories that are Under-Deployed (need more stock) or Over-Deployed (tying up excess cash).
   Scenario Overrides: Interactive simulation to see how changing DOC targets affects working capital requirements.

### 🔬 7. Data Exploration & Export
   Flexible SKU Explorer: Custom scatter plots to correlate any two metrics (e.g., Sales vs Margin, or Cost vs Gap Units).
   Pareto (80/20) Analysis: Automatically isolates the top 20% of SKUs driving 80% of total purchase value.
   Full Data Grid: Granular table with advanced search, color-coded status alerts, and one-click CSV export.
